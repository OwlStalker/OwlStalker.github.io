Flying Bees LLC

Flying Bees LLC was established May 10th, 2012. We are a family business that raise honey bees and provide other services. You can be assured that when we harvest honey that there is nothing added such as preservatives or additional ingredients and that what comes off the frames is what you pay for. We also raise and sell Queen bees which is how beekeepers are able to raise hives. Other services we offer are hive removals and pollination services. If you have a nest of bees flying around somewhere on the property and want them removed, we can give you a quote for removal. Pollination services are used by farmers all the time which is when we bring our family of bees to a piece of farmed land and they get to work pollinating our clients' crops.
