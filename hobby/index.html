About

My name is David Kravchuk. I am a senior at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. Ever since I was a young kid I had grown a passion for being a nerd and a love for cars. More so, a love for cars. It kinda ran through the family if I'm honest. I loved to work on a salvaged cars that my dad bought to repair. It would be the weekends where we would get so much done since that was the only time he was home. Ever since I started college back in 2018 I don't have the time or money to continue working on any project car that I want sadly. Nonetheless I can probably name every car that you show a picture of because, growing up, I made a habit of knowing every car to ever release.

Here is the dream car that I hope to own one day if god gives.
