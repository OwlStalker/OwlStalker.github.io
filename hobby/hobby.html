About

My name is David Kravchuk. I am a senior at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. Ever since I was a young kid I had grown a passion for being a nerd and a love for cars. More so, a love for cars. It kinda runs through the family if I'm honest. I loved to work on a salvaged cars that my dad bought to repair. It would be the weekends where we would get so much done since that was the only time he was home. Ever since I started college back in 2018 I don't have the time or money to continue working on any project car that I want sadly. Nonetheless I can probably name every car that you show a picture of because, growing up, I made a habit of knowing every car to ever release.

Here is the dream car that I hope to own one day if god gives.

Home

My love for cars started when I was young kid. The idea of riding fast and repairing cars with my pops was enough to get me to love it. My dad is also a semi-truck driver so we would always work on that first when it broke down. As a kid we would always be doing something with our hands around the house, preferably on a car.

This is the bad boy that we would constantly be fixing.

First Car

In 2018 I bought a car off a salvage auction. It was a 2010 BMW 335i. It was my first non-front wheel drive car, and it was a lot of fun to drive. I eventually listed it for sale because it was not a daily driver for the work I was doing at the time.

First Car Wreck

In 2021 my heart broke when I was crossing an intersection and a drunk person skimmed the front of my car, barely tboning me. I then hired a lawyer to handle my case and eventually got the insurance company to pay out my car since they were hesitant at first.

The last picture was me saying goodbye to her out in the tow yard.

Latest Car

Recently Toyota anounced a new 2023 GR Corolla model. It features a 1.6-liter 3-cylinder direct/port-injected turbocharged engine, which generates a mighty 300 hp and 273 lb-ft of torque. If this car ever makes its way to the salvage auction, you best believe I'm bidding for it.

Tuner Car

I've always wanted to tune my own car one day. The car I would choose to tune would be a 2016 Toyota GT86 with a swapped CL65 V10 engine in it.
