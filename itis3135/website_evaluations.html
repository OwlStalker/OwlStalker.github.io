Website Evaluations


Bleeping Computer


The Bleeping Computer news source is a blog site that keeps up with latest security threats, technology news, ways to stay protected online, and how to use their computers more efficiently.


  • Contrast: The contrast of the site is solid, with the colors including different shades of blue as well as white, green, and black.

  • Repetition: The repetition is constant throughout the page. The font sizes for each individual post have a consistent form and are easy to scroll through if you want to find a particular article.

  • Alignment: The alignment is uniform with every element and is easy to navigate through.

  • Proximity: The spacing used in this design creates a sense of inclusion. It gives me a feeling that all the latest updates and bugs are found here as each article has links to other resources about that topic.

Arngren


This site is some marketplace for kids motor toys and looks to be a scam possibly.


  • Contrast: The contrast for this website is absolute garbage. It spans a multitude of different colors as well as font colors which makes it hard to even read anything.

  • Repetition: The only repetition I see on this page is the constant outlining of products using a black border. It doesn't distinguish anything apart from other products so I don't see what the use was for that.

  • Alignment: There is no such thing as alignment on this site. They've got text running into other text, all of the pictures are different sizes, . The more I look at it, the more I think this was a real attempt at making amazon 2.0.

  • Proximity: I'll admit that every picture has text related to it which is enclosed within a box with black borders. To be serious though this page is a joke. It looks like a collage of all the things my elementary teacher told me to glue to a page that I thought was cool or that I wanted.
