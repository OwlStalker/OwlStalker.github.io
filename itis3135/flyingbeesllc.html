Project Design

Project Overview

  • This website is for my brothers’ bee business. It will serve as a way for him to sell his pure honey, sell queen bees to raise other hives, remove hives from people’s homes that they don’t want around, as well as provide a pollination service for farmers.
  • Customers that want to purchase quality honey by different sizes (jars). People that want a hive removed from their property. Customers that want to buy queen bees to raise their own hives at home or for a business. Customers or Farmers that want to pollinate their crops for a price.
  • The overall purpose of the website is to provide four different services related to bees.

Site Map

Home Page

  • Purpose of the page is to give a brief description about the business as serve as the home page.
  • Audience: Customers
  • Content: About us, Location, Hours of operation, Pictures of business.

Honey Page

  • Purpose: To allow customers to select the amount of honey they would like to purchase.
  • Audience: Customers
  • Content: Submission Form, Name, Address, Email, Amount of honey to be purchased. (Pint, Quart, Gallon, Bucket (5 Gallons)).
  • Notes: Validate the amount wanted compared to available stock.

Queen Bee Page

  • Purpose of the page is to allow customers to purchase queen bees in order to raise their own hives for the price of $25 dollars.
  • Audience: Customers
  • Content: Submission Form, Name, Phone Number, Email.
  • Notes: Validate amount wanted compared to available stock.

Hive Removal Page

  • Purpose of the page is to provide a bee hive removal service in which a customer would call us out to remove a colony of bees near or on their property.
  • Audience: Customers
  • Content: Submission Form, Name, Address, Phone Number, Email.
  • Notes: Quotes for removal depend on the location of the bees.

Pollination Services Page

  • The purpose of this page is to allow businesses to inquire information about us bringing out our hives for a set amount of days to pollinate their crops.
  • Audience: Customers/Businesses
  • Content: Submission Form, Acreage of land, Name, Address, Email, Phone Number. Quotes will be given based on the information we receive about the inquiry.
  • Notes: Make sure amount of acres is securely covered by the amount of hives provided (Lands that are too large for pollination).

Dynamic Functionality

  • This website will most likely only use this for the pictures of the business which will be located on the home page. All other pages will include a simple form in which customers fill out and we receive the information of either how to contact them, amount purchased, or a basic understanding of what service they require of us.
